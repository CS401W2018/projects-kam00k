Structural Tags

What goes in the header?

Generally things that remain the same throughout the website's UI.
Things such as logos, search bars and navigation links are examples.

What goes in the nav section?

Things particular to site navigation such as tables of contents, indexes and menus
are usually in this section. It often used within the header tags, but does not have to be.
It is generally bad practice for this section to contain links to other websites.

What goes in main?

The main tags contain the focal information a given webpage contains.
This is the content the webpage was made for the purpose of displaying.

What goes in the footer?

The footer contains information about the website such as copyright,
legal and privacy notices, contact information, and sometimes cookies preferences.
