How do I make my own pixelart?

Unlike many other art media, the barrier to entry for making pixelart is rock-bottom. After all, you're just painting with squares! All you need to get started is a paint program and a mouse.

That said, getting good at making pixelart takes practice, just like any other skill. The sky is the limit for how skilled you want to become. Luckily, you are on the internet, which is absolutely brimming with tutorials and resources of every kind to help you make your blocky visions come to life.
