What Is Pixelart?

Pixelart is a term that refers to a type of art style where images are drawn using a limited number of pixels and, often, a limited colorspace. The term "pixelart" is actually quite new, and has only come to refer to this art style relatively recently. Until the 1990s, almost all computer graphics were done in this style simply because of technological limitations.

Though these limitations have long since been surpassed, pixelart still remains an extremely popular style used in the development of video games to this day. Even if you are fairly young, odds are some of your favorite video games are pixelart games, in some form or another. Far from being considered "retro", it still holds its own. The advent of modern color spaces has only expanded what is possible with this iconic art style.
