Welcome!

This is a special page dedicated to the vast, vibrant world of pixelart. Here you can find all sorts of information about pixelart, including its history, its variety, and even some resources on how to make your own! You can also use the contact form on this website to get in touch with me, either for more information, or to commission some pixelart from yours truly (shameless plug)!

As you will soon see, pixelart is a thriving and varied art style used throughout the art and game development sphere. Its sharp, clean borders and bright color palettes add a quintessentially "digital" touch to any project.
